# MECO Tokenomics

## Overview
MonyCoin (MECO) is a lightweight utility token built on the Solana blockchain.  
Its economic model is designed ليكون بسيط، مستقر، وسهل الاستخدام داخل منظومة MECO Wallet.

---

## Token Information
- **Name:** MonyCoin  
- **Symbol:** MECO  
- **Network:** Solana  
- **Decimals:** 9  
- **Type:** SPL Token  

---

## Supply
